Ayah - 3
ٱلَّذِينَ يُؤۡمِنُونَ بِٱلۡغَيۡبِ وَيُقِيمُونَ ٱلصَّلَوٰةَ وَمِمَّا رَزَقۡنَٰهُمۡ يُنفِقُونَ
Who believe in the unseen, establish prayer, and spend out of what We have provided for them

ٱلَّذِينَ يُؤۡمِنُونَ بِٱلۡغَيۡبِ (Those who believe in the unseen)
In our times, we believe things that can be proven scientifically. But this philosphy falls over itself, like most of the human emotions are unseen like love, appreciation etc. 
Allah uses the present tenses, it suggests continuaty, struggle meaning incomplete. This people have faith, means that they keeps working on their iman meaning their iman goes down and high. But they keep working to bring it back up. 

What does it mean to believe in the unseen?
There are things that we can't see like Allah, angels, the miracles. But, there is also something deep that they see a kind of reality that other people cannot see.
For example, when someone is backbitting someone people don't feel anything but a person with Iman will feel something in his heart. This is living iman in the unseen.
So, there is a difference between facts about Islam because (it is quantitative) but taqwa is qualitative or Iman in the unseen is qualitiative that is why there iman goes down and up.

One of the most important quality of iman in the unseen is hope. A true momin never gets depressed. No matter how hard time gets we should always have iman in Allah.

Now, when you have the reality of unseen how will you keep it refresh. So, the next part.

وَيُقِيمُونَ ٱلصَّلَوٰةَ → They establish prayer. Establishing prayer means to pray together to establish prayer with other as well. So it means to go to the masjid and pray. There is difference between praying the prayer and establishing prayer. Salah comes from Silah which means connection.

وَمِمَّا رَزَقۡنَٰهُمۡ يُنفِقُونَ
Ma is more vastness. Whatever we provided them (like some people were provided with health, mind, money, sons daughters). so everyone rizq is different and the spend from that rizq tht Allah provided them.
Nafiqa is lizards hole. they would make two openings. if trouble is from one side then get out from the other. Similarly kings have holes (tunnel) from which they can get out if their castle gets invaded. So, this is the concept behind infaq. That we deposit in this world and we will get the return in the next life. The idea of spending in path of Allah means to spend and you will get return in the next life and this cannot be acheived if we not have believe in the unseeen. 

وَمِمَّا رَزَقۡنَٰهُمۡ → Allah mentions we provided them because we forget that everything we have is given to us by Allah. Allah also uses past tense for Rizq. One benefit for this is that Allah has already given and we should not think when Allah will give then we will donate.
