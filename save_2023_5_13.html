Ayah - 2
ذَٰلِكَ ٱلۡكِتَٰبُ لَا رَيۡبَۛ فِيهِۛ هُدٗى لِّلۡمُتَّقِينَ
This is the Book about which there is no doubt, a guidance for those conscious of Allah

ٱلۡكِتَٰبُ 
Kataba in modern Arabic means to write, but in old times pen was not very common. Back in the day people used to carve in stones or tablets and the problem with that is once you have written something then it is not possible for you to edit it. So by using the word Kitab. Allah reveals a profound knowledge that Quran is once set and there is no room for editting. So the order the placement and everything is how it is meant to be. 

Difference between Quran and Kitab


رَيۡبَۛ Several word use for doubt like other words (Shak)
But other flavors of رَيۡبَۛ includes.
When the person is disturbed or shaken up by inside. Like a doubt that bothers you.
For example, you see a car and you can't determine what model is then that is not رَيۡبَۛ.

لِّلۡمُتَّقِينَ (Al Mutaqeen) comes from word wiqaya. 
Excessive hyper protection. Some kind of horse is called wiqaya horse. For instance when a horse loses it's horse shoe then it walk pretty carefully for the step it take because its feet have become sensitive.
